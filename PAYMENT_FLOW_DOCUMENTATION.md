## Understanding Orders

### What is an Order?

An **Order** represents a transaction where you:
- **Sell** materials to a **Party** (customer)
- **Buy** raw materials from a **Supplier**

### Key Numbers in an Order

Every order has these important amounts:

1. **Original Total (â‚¹)** = `originalWeight Ã— originalRate`

   - This is the **selling price** to your customer (party)

3. **Additional Cost (â‚¹)**

   - Example: â‚¹2,000 for truck loading

## How Payments Work on Orders

### What are Partial Payments?

**Partial Payments** are payments you make to suppliers for raw materials. They are stored in the `partialPayments` array on each order.

## How Ledger Links to Orders and Parties

### When You Add an Expense Entry (Debit) with Supplier

**What Happens:**
1. Ledger entry is created (expense)
2. System finds all **unpaid orders** for that supplier
3. Automatically distributes the expense amount across those orders
4. Creates partial payments on orders (marked "From Ledger")
5. Orders are marked as paid if amount is sufficient

**Example:**
```
You add expense: â‚¹30,000 for Supplier A

Supplier A has:
- Order 1: Original Total â‚¹20,000 (unpaid)
- Order 2: Original Total â‚¹15,000 (unpaid)

System automatically:
- Adds â‚¹20,000 payment to Order 1 (fully paid âœ…)
- Adds â‚¹10,000 payment to Order 2 (partially paid)
```

### When You Add an Income Entry (Credit) with Party Name

**What Happens:**
1. Ledger entry is created (income)
2. A payment record is automatically created for that party
3. This payment is linked to the ledger entry
4. Payment appears in party details (read-only)

**Example:**
```
You add income: â‚¹70,000 with Party Name "ABC Company"

System automatically:
- Creates ledger credit entry: â‚¹70,000
- Creates party payment: â‚¹70,000 for "ABC Company"
- Links them together
```

### Important Rules

- **Ledger payments are read-only** - You cannot edit or delete them
- **Manual payments are editable** - You can change or remove them
- **If you change ledger entry** - Linked payments update automatically
- **If you delete ledger entry** - Linked payments are removed

1. **Orders** = Your business transactions (buying and selling)

5. **Ledger + Party** = Automatically records customer payment
